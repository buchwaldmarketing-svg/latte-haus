Our Story

Brewing Something Special for Oak Cliff

Latte Haus was born from a simple idea — that Oak Cliff deserves a coffee shop where everyone feels like family. Founded by Emily, a passionate young entrepreneur, we opened our doors on W Kiest Blvd to bring handcrafted drinks, warm energy, and a beautifully designed space to the neighborhood.

Whether you're grabbing your morning cold brew, settling in for a midday matcha, or catching up with friends over one of our seasonal specials — this is your space. We're not just making coffee. We're building community, one cup at a time.
